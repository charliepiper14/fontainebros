What Works — Creative Principles

  • Anti-guru positioning — "We only make money when you do" is the strongest brand differentiator. Always lead with this.
  • Short-form founder video (60–90 sec) — Direct income results hook. "Dylan £800pm video" achieved £1.52 CPL, best in 3-year account history.
  • Outcome specificity — "£800/month", "first deal in 7 days", "40k/month deal sourcing" dramatically outperform vague benefit claims.
  • Urgency + value stack — Flash sale format (£12k value → £399) with countdown creates natural purchase pressure.
  • Real member testimonials — David Butmaro testimonial converts, even at higher CPL, because it provides authentic proof.
  • Video always beats static — Confirmed across all three accounts and multiple time periods. Never rely on image ads for primary spend.

What Fails — Critical Lessons

  • Lead gen hooks on purchase campaigns — THE SAD TRUTH drove curiosity → form fills. On a purchase page it drives curiosity → no immediate buy. Never cross-use creative between objectives.
  • Long-form VSL — At £399 this is an impulse + conviction purchase. VSLs add friction. Short punchy video + direct CTA converts better.
  • Static image ads for purchase — BFCM 2025 image ads spent £2,408 at 0.39–0.80x ROAS. Property education requires video trust-building for cold traffic to purchase.
  • Overnight budget explosions — Never increase daily budget more than 20–30% every 2–3 days. This is what killed the primary account.
  • Running same creative too long — CPL data shows clear fatigue pattern. Creative must rotate every 3–4 weeks at scale.

F#1 Ad Copy Analysis — Educational Angle

The 5 educational copy variants currently running in F#1 are awareness-stage, low-friction hooks designed to generate leads from people earlier in the property research journey. Headlines like "Curious About Property?" and "Learn How Property Deals Are Found" attract a broader but potentially lower-intent audience vs the "anti-guru" direct income hooks. At £4.44 CPL this is working well for volume — but the quality of these leads vs the THE SAD TRUTH leads needs to be monitored via sales team close rate. Recommendation: A/B test the educational hooks against the proven anti-guru angle on the same ad set to compare lead quality at equivalent CPL.
